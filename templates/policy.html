Service Guarantee & Policy

CampusFit is committed to providing a reliable and valuable fitness experience for all users.

Service Guarantee

CampusFit aims to provide structured workout plans, a functional dashboard, and a smooth user experience. While results depend on individual effort, we guarantee access to all features included in your membership.

Subscription Policy

CampusFit currently operates on a membership model. Users must activate their subscription to access dashboard features and workout plans.

Refund Policy

Since this version of CampusFit uses a simulated subscription system, no real payments are processed. In future versions, refund policies will be clearly defined and applied where applicable.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for performing exercises safely and consulting a professional if needed. CampusFit is not liable for injuries resulting from improper use of the workouts.

Future Updates

CampusFit may introduce new features, improvements, and policies as the platform grows. Users will be notified of major updates affecting their experience.
