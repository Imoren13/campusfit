CampusFit Services

CampusFit is a subscription-based digital fitness service for students who want structured workout plans, a simple dashboard, and guided daily training.

Membership Access

Members receive access to the CampusFit dashboard, workout plan selection, daily workout tracking, and progress tools.

Workout Plans

Users can choose from six structured workout plans based on goals such as muscle building, fat loss, beginner fitness, home training, strength, and athletic performance.

Today’s Workout

The dashboard automatically maps each plan to the current weekday, so users know what workout to complete each day.

Workout Details

Members can open a detailed workout page, check off completed exercises, and view a circular progress indicator for the day’s workout.

Subscription Model

CampusFit currently uses one membership tier that provides full access to the service. Payment gateway integration may be added later, but the current version uses a simulated subscription activation flow.
