Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about CampusFit memberships, workout plans, subscriptions, and dashboard features.

What is CampusFit?

CampusFit is a digital fitness membership website that provides structured workout plans and a member dashboard.

Who is CampusFit for?

CampusFit is designed mainly for college students who want a simple way to follow workout plans and stay consistent.

How many workout plans are offered?

CampusFit currently offers six workout plans, including muscle building, fat loss, beginner, home workout, strength, and athletic performance plans.

Can I change my selected plan?

Yes. Users can return to the plan selection page and choose a different workout plan at any time.

Does CampusFit track completed exercises?

Yes. The Workout Details page allows users to check off completed exercises and view workout completion progress.

Is payment processing active?

The current version uses a simulated subscription activation flow. A payment gateway may be added later.
